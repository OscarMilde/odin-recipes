Katsu Curry

Ingredients

For the chicken katsu...

  • Chicken breasts
  • breadcrumbs
  • A beaten egg
  • Oil for frying

For the curry sauce...

  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Japanese curry cubes
  • Brown sugar

Steps

For the Chicken Katsu...

  1. season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge each chicken breast in all-purpose flour, ensuring they're evenly coated.
  3. Fry in oil until golden and cooked through.

For the sauce

  1. Saute minced garlic and ginger in oil
  2. Add soy sauce, cubes and brown sugar to taste
  3. Gradually add water until sauce is smooth and cubes have simmered.

To serve

  1. Slice chicken katsu into strips
  2. Served over cooked white rice
  3. Pour sauce over chicken and rice
  4. Optionally, serve with salad

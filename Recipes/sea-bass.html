Salt-Baked Sea Bass

Ingredients

  • 300g New Potatoes
  • 100g Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Bunch of Thyme (50g)
  • Whole Sea Bass (About 1.75kg), Gutted And Scaled
  • 2 Lemons
  • 2.5kg Coarse Sea Salt
  • 200g Cavolo Nero
  • 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1 Red Chilli
  • 5 Tbsp Extra-Virgin Olive Oil

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Halve new potatoes and tomatoes, toss in olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper, and roast for 35 mins.
  3. Prep sea bass with salt, lemon, and thyme.
  4. Create a salt crust for the fish.
  5. Add the fish and roast for 25 mins.
  6. Steam Cavolo Nero and season.
  7. Make chili oil by mixing chopped chili, olive oil, lemon zest, and juice.
  8. Break the salt crust, serve fish with chili oil, and enjoy with potatoes and Cavolo Nero.

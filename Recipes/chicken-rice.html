Chicken Rice with Garlic Yoghurt and Burnt Lemon

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 3 shallots
    • 6 garlic cloves
  • 500g cherry tomatoes
  • 400g green olives
  • 1tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 200g basmati rice
  • 600ml chicken stock
  • 2 lemons
  • 250g yoghurt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 juiced lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees.
  2. Debone and salt the chicken.
  3. Heat a pan with olive oil, fry chicken until golden, and set aside.
  4. Cook shallots, then add garlic, tomatoes, spices, and olives.
  5. Add rice and chicken stock, bake at 200 degrees for 35 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and rest for 10 minutes with the lid on.
  7. Make dressing with yogurt, garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice.
  8. Caramelize 4 lemon halves in a hot pan.
  9. Garnish chicken with herbs, yogurt dressing, and burnt lemons.
